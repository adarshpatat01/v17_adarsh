POS - Point of Sales Client Feedback Odoo Apps

POS Client Feedback Odoo App transforms into the retail landscape by enhancing the point-of-sale experience through customer feedback. This Odoo app allows users to configure feedback emails, ensuring that customers receive a prompt email upon completing a POS order payment. Customers can conveniently provide ratings and reviews, contributing valuable insights to businesses. Also prioritizes customer preferences by offering an unsubscribe option for receiving feedback emails.

Features

Customer Feedback & Rating for POS Order

Get feedback and rating from customer for point of sale order.

Subscribe/Unsubscribe from Feedback Email

Customer can subscribe or unsubscribe from feedback email anytime.
Demo Request Video Tutorial

Customer Feedback Configuration

User need to enable "Feedback Email" option to receive feedback email.

When POS order paid with selected customer will get email notification for feedback.

Feedback Email

Customer will get an email for feedback, also need to click button as shown image to give a feedback.

Rating & Feedback Form

Customer can select ratings and can add feedback and click on "SUBMIT" button.

On submit of feedback success message will appear.

Feedback & Rating on POS Order

POS manager can see customer feedback and rating under "Feedback & Rating" tab on POS order.

Unsubscribe for Feedback

User can unsubscribe from receiving feedback and rating email by clicking "Unsubscribe" button on email.

Customer can see email is unsubscribed and "Feedback Email" option is disable automatically on customer view.
